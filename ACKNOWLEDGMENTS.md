### Taylor Simone Ledward Boseman

We acknowledge and honor the profound words spoken by **Taylor Simone Ledward Boseman** at the posthumous induction of **Chadwick Boseman's star** on the Hollywood Walk of Fame in **Los Angeles, California on November 21, 2025**.

Her words, spoken into our wind, our cultural ecosystem, and our creative ecology, carry forward the legacy of Chadwick Boseman and remind us of the enduring power of love, art, and purpose.

#### Source Attribution

This acknowledgment recognizes the coverage and documentation provided by **Fashionsizzle** in their article covering this historic moment.

## About This Acknowledgment

The SteppingStones project believes in honoring those who speak truth into our collective consciousness. Taylor Simone Ledward Boseman's words at this ceremony represent stepping stones for future generations—moments of profound articulation that guide us forward.

Chadwick Boseman's legacy, carried forward by his wife and celebrated through this star on the Walk of Fame, continues to inspire creators, artists, and dreamers worldwide.
